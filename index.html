What is HackOverflow?

HackOverflow is a 12-hour hackathon hosted by Stanford Women in Computer Science (WiCS), focused on building with awesome hardware. We’ll be providing fun things to hack on, mentors, workshops, swag, and food!


We encourage people of all skill levels to participate, although it is recommended that you have some experience programming, whether it be from taking 106A or fiddling around on your own. Whether you've never been to a hackathon and want to try one out or just want to experiment with hardware, this is the right place for you!
